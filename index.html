302

Death Thrash Metal from Sungai Buloh, Malaysia

302 emerged in 2023 from the vibrant metal scene in Sungai Buloh, Malaysia. The band gained momentum following their explosive debut performance with BLACKFIRE and BUNUHDIRI at Timeless Studio Damansara Perdana. Quickly amassing a dedicated local following, 302 released their first single, "For Freedom" (2024), showcasing their unique blend of classic thrash influences and modern groove metal sounds. Their powerful riffs and intense performances create an unforgettable headbanging experience for metal enthusiasts.
