302

Death Thrash Metal from Sungai Buloh

Formed in 2023, 302 is a death thrash metal band straight outta Sungai Buloh, Malaysia. The band made noise right from the jump, debuting live with BLACKFIRE and BUNUHDIRI at Timeless Studio, Damansara Perdana. With a sound that smashes classic thrash vibes into modern groove metal aggression, they dropped their first single "For Freedom" in 2024 — and it hit hard. Known for tight riffs, raw energy, and no-nonsense performances, 302 isn’t here to play nice — they’re here to rip stages.
