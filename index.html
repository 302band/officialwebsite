302

Death Thrash Metal From Malaysia

Formed in 2023, 302 is a death/thrash metal band hailing from Sungai Buloh, Malaysia. Blending old-school thrash intensity with modern groove and aggression, the band made their explosive debut alongside legendary acts BLACK FIRE and BUNUHDIRI at Timeless Studio, Damansara Perdana.

With razorsharp riffs, relentless rhythms, and a commanding stage presence, 302 quickly established a cult following in the underground scene. Their debut single "For Freedom" (2024) captures the band's raw energy and commitment to crafting metal that hits hard both musically and thematically.

Currently in the works: Their upcoming debut EP, which promises to be a sonic assault influenced by titans like Sodom, Sepultura, and Megadeth while carving their own distinct sound in Southeast Asia's metal landscape.
